URGENT PSA Traditional Kubernetes Ingress controllers reach End of Life (EOL) in March 2026. The Vanguard protocol mandates a complete transition to Istio Ingress Gateway to ensure operational continuity.

Why Istio Ingress Gateway?

Moving beyond NGINX Ingress is like upgrading from a reliable sedan to a futuristic starship. Here's why Istio wins:

  • Unified Control Plane: Single point of management for security and traffic.
  • Advanced Traffic Steering: Native support for Canary and Blue/Green.
  • mTLS Everywhere: Built-in zero-trust encryption.
  • L7 Observability: Deep request insights without sidecar injection.
Architectural Patterns: Shared vs. Dedicated

Shared Ingress Gateway

Standardized multi-tenant ingress cluster.

  • Resource optimized (one LB)
  • Centralized policy management
  • Risk: Noisy neighbor interference

Dedicated Ingress Gateway

Sovereign gateway per application/team.

  • Total blast radius isolation
  • Independent scaling and cycles
  • Risk: Increased cloud provider costs
