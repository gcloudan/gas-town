Why Istio Ingress?

Moving beyond NGINX Ingress is like upgrading from a reliable sedan to a futuristic starship. Here's why Istio wins:

  • Unified Control Plane: Manage security, traffic, and observability in one place.
  • Advanced Traffic Steering: Canary deployments, mirroring, and circuit breaking are first-class citizens.
  • mTLS Everywhere: Zero-trust networking is built-in, not bolted on.
  • Observability: Deep insights into every request without changing your app code.
Shared vs. Dedicated Gateways

Shared Gateways

One Gateway to rule them all. Best for small clusters or when cost is a primary concern.

Pros: Lower resource overhead, simpler initial setup.

Dedicated Gateways

A Gateway for every team or application. Best for high-traffic apps and strict isolation.

Pros: Better blast radius control, independent scaling, team autonomy.
