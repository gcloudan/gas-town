Architectural Strategy: Shared vs. Dedicated

The choice between shared and dedicated gateways defines your operational complexity and blast radius.

Option A: Shared Gateway

Consolidate multiple namespaces into a single high-performance gateway cluster.

  • Efficiency: Maximum resource utilization, minimum cloud LB costs.
  • Governance: Centralized certificate management and global policies.
  • Trade-off: Higher blast radius; misconfiguration affects all tenants.

Option B: Dedicated Gateway

Deploy sovereign gateways per-team or per-application for maximum isolation.

  • Sovereignty: Teams manage their own scaling, lifecycle, and security.
  • Blast Radius: Zero interference between application traffic profiles.
  • Trade-off: Higher operational footprint; increased LoadBalancer costs.
